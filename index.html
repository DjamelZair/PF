Welcome!

Hi there, and welcome to my personal webpage! I'm Djamel, a machine learning enthusiast with experience in healthcare and banking. This page acts complementary to my CV to share more about my work and projects and interests.

Personally, I'm mostly fascinated by the potential of Large Language Models (LLMs). How techniques like Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) enhance data privacy in sectors like finance and healthcare. The ways prompt design, fine-tuning, and Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF) help reduce bias and promote fairness. Moreover, I'm excited of the new possibilities for inclusiveness, especially for individuals with disabilities, and how it allows people to focus on more creative and meaningful work by automating monotonous and repetitive tasks.

On this page, I’m excited to share some examples I’ve worked on and the expertise I’ve gained along the way, showing my proficiency in python, knowledge of A.I. developments and data analytics.

Side note : The projects I am most proud of are under NDA's, but I hope this portfolio shows I know and can apply the concepts. Curious? I would love to chat about what is not here (yet).
