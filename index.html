LLM

Large Language Models are trained on huge amounts of human written text. This means the bias that people can have, can transfer into the LLM.

We investigated how RoBERTa, GPT2-QA, and BART might perpetuate stereotypes, reflect unequal or negative sentiment, or show limited diversity in their answers.

We examined bias across social dimensions such as race, ethnicity, religion, age, nationality, and sexuality.

Keywords: BBQ Dataset, BART, GPT2-QA, Bias Classification, Untrained Automatic Evaluation Scores, ROUGE-L, BERTScore, etc.
